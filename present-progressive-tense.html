The Present Progressive Tense

Some people find the tense s p o o k y . Like a monster under a bed, after this lesson, that fear will simply disappear!

The present progressive is formed by 'estar' + 'present participle'

The form of 'estar' is in the present tense and chosen based on off who's talking

To form the present participle, ask the following questions

1. Is the verb regular?

2. Is it an AR verb or ER/IR verb?

2. Is the verb a double vowel (stem ends in a vowel) or an IR verb that stem changes in the present? (if not, go back to YES)

To form the present particple for regular AR verbs...

  1. Remove the 'ar'
  2. Replace with 'ando'>

    3. Examples: hablando, estudiando, golpeando

    Done!

To form the present particple for regular ER/IR verbs...

  1. Remove the 'er' or 'ir'
  2. Replace with 'iendo'>

    3. Examples: comiendo, poniendo, teniendo

    Done!

To form the present particple for double vowel verbs...

  1. Remove the ending
  2. Replace with yendo>

    3. Examples: leyendo, creyendo, incluyendo

    Two more irregulars: Ir and Seguir

    Ir = "yendo" and Seguir = "siguiendo"

    Done!

To form the present particple for stem changing IR verbs...

E:IE or E:I verbs only stem change from E:I for the present participle, O:UE verbs only stem change to O:U

  1. Stem change the second-to-last-vowel accordingly
  2. Replace the ending with "iendo"

    3. Examples: dormir ---> durmiendo, servir ---> sirviendo
