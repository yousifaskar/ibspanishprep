Translate the following sentences into Spanish
Do not use punctuation at all, except for accents and atildes. Include subjects (yo, ella, etc). Do not use IOPS or DOPS
  1. It's possible that my brother can run.
  2. I want my friend to buy a ball for me.
  3. I hope my house is beautiful.
  4. She will leave as soon as she buys a car.
  5. She is sad that I went to the party yesterday.
  6. We doubted that my brother did his homework.
  7. I will bring two books in case I finish my book.
  8. It was good that my dad only visited Cuba.
